Weather and Latitude: Global Positional Influence on Observed Weather

Various weather measurements were made at more than 500 weather stations randomly distributed globally and accessed utilizing the OpenWeatherMap API.

Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and WindSpeed were plotted against Latitude utilizing MatPlotLib to generate the images accesible on these HTML pages.

All of the Data utilized to construct these pages and the conclusions derived therein are accessible in the appropriately named tab.
