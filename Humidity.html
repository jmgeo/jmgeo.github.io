Humidity

Temperature and Humidity are less correlable. Humidity in aggregate is higher near the Equator and less near the Poles, but a correlation in the mid latitudes is clearly dependent on additional factors other than global position (elevation and proximity to large bodies of water would be two obvious and significant contributors to measured humidity that are not correlable to latitude).
